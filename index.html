Meet the Teacher

This online course is designed and taught by Yogi Ram (ERYT 500, YACEP, 40,000 hours teaching experience). Yogi Ram is a renowned yoga teacher from India and the Director of Arhanta Yoga Ashrams in India and The Netherlands.

Born and raised in India his yoga education started from the age of 8 years as part of his school education. He has been teaching since 1998 and he is teaching yoga teacher training courses since 2009. He has trained over 3,000 yoga teachers in last 10 years.

Ram has studied Yin yoga from Paul Grilley (founder, Yin Yoga).
